Open Diagnosis Foundation

using AI to provide free medical diagnostics

How?

The Open Diagnosis Foundation is a non-profit aimed at using artificial intelligence to democratize global access to healthcare. Our image analysis tools diagnose various forms of cancer and chronic illness with up to 98% accuracy - and we want to give them to the world for free. Our algorithms can process thousands of scans a second - a traditional radiologist who has had almost ten years of education and training can only process one scan every ten minutes. Often, our algorithms are more accurate than radiologists.

"Open Diagnosis Foundation is an incredible step forward in providing vital diagnostic resources to areas that otherwise had no chance"

-Maxwell Qu M.D.
Royal Brisbane and Women's Hospital, Queensland Australia

What makes the Open Diagnosis Foundation so important?

The accessibility gap for radiology-based diagnostics is startling - the country of Nigeria has a population of 190 million people yet only 60 radiologists. On the other hand, a single hospital in the US, Massachusetts General Hospital, has 192 radiologists. This situation is not uncommon. Liberia has 2 radiologists for the entire country.

Improved access to diagnostics can lower the global cost of healthcare, provide access to resource deprived regions, and diagnose illness earlier. Artificial Intelligence improves diagnostic accuracy, reducing incorrect diagnoses and saves lives.

The world is at a crucial junction in medical history - either these tools will be free and accessible, or they will be heavily commercialized and available only to those with the means to afford them. Open Diagnosis Foundations mission is to ensure that everyone is able to access the algorithms that will usher in the next era of medical innovation. All donations are used for research, development and, outreach, to improve the number of conditions diagnosed and ensure these tools are put into the hands of people that need them the most.

The Open Diagnosis Foundation is completely HIPPA compliant - no medical data or medical images ever leave the user's computer. This is what makes the tool provided by Open Dianosis Foundation so powerful.



The tool is intended to be downloaded locally allowing users to disconnect their computer from the internet, this is known as "air-gapping", wherein the tool will still continue to function. Air-gapping is the gold standard in data security and privacy. Whats more, air-gapping has the added benefit that these tools can still be used in regions that don't have reliable or high-bandwidth internet connections. We recommend that all users download the tools locally, and periodically check our website each month to see if newer versions of our diagnostic tools are available. If there is a newer version, please download it as soon as possible.
